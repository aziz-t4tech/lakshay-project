About Us

Company History

The idea behind the incorporation of Lakshya Complete Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was conceived by Mr. Sandeep Tiwari, who had been toying with this idea for the past few years. The company was incorporated with a wide objective of manufacturing, fabricating, designing, assembling, and dealing in all kinds of furniture, plants and machinery, electric fittings, installation, and implementation of computer labs, and all types of turnkey projects.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, innovative, and sustainable solutions in the field of manufacturing, ICT projects, and turnkey solutions while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be a leading company in IT solutions, infrastructure, and manufacturing by continuously evolving with technology and client needs.
